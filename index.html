Interested in our services? We’re here to help! We want to know your needs exactly so that we can provide the perfect solution. Let us know what you want and we’ll do our best to help.

At New Vision Painting & Decorating we pride ourselves on going that extra mile for our customers and being able to offer the highest quality of workmanship to our growing and diverse client base. We have extensive knowledge and skill to be able to complete any aspect of the painting and decorating trade to the highest standard. Using a combination of traditional and modern methods we aim to provide a first class service and finish for you. We provide commercial and residential painting and decorating services in Brisbane and its surrounding suburbs. We understand the importance of the fact that we are going to be working in your personal space – whether at home or your place of work. Our decorators treat your house as we would our own. We are there to work around you, to work to your time frame and your individual circumstances and so to make the experience as efficient and smooth as we can.
