New Vision Painting & Decorating is a proud member of the Master Painters Association, and we adhere to all Australian standards on every job. We are also fully insured, with workers’ compensation and public liability insurance certificates available upon request.

About Us

At New Vision Painting & Decorating we pride ourselves on going that extra mile for our customers and being able to offer the highest quality of workmanship to our growing and diverse client base. we have extensive knowledge and skill to be able to complete any aspect of the painting and decorating trade to the highest standard. Using a combination of traditional and modern methods we aim to provide a first class service and finish for you. We provide commercial and residential painting and decorating services in Brisbane and its surrounding suburbs. We understand the importance of the fact that we are going to be working in your personal space – whether at home or your place of work. Our decorators treat your house as we would our own. We are there to work around you, to work to your time frame and your individual circumstances and so to make the experience as efficient and smooth as we can. We have a team of highly skilled professionals ready to provide commercial and residential painting and decorating services. Our steady expansion and our success is due to the care taken on each job to make sure our customers are fully satisfied with the work done. We are fully insured with Public Liability Insurance. We work 7 days a week and do quotes in the evenings as well, so we can always find a time that suits you best.
